Hello! I am currently in my 2nd year at UC Riverside and am pursuing a major in communcation science and disorders.

Working with children is my specialty. I am passionate about helping others to be able to communicate their needs.That is why I am on my way to become a SLPA (Assistant) and later earn my Masters to become an SLP. After seeing my younger brother go through therapy sessions with speech, I was fascinated and wanted to learn more. I believe there needs to be more attention to what Speech Therapy is and why it is important.There are many disabilities and causes for needing speech therapy and being able to communicate effectively is just as important as overall care.

I am excited to work and am open for internships.I have previously volunteered at Loma Linda Hosptial and the Temecula Valley High School speech program for 2 years and I hope to be able to contribute more at clinics, hospitals, or schools.
